Start training with a personal plan that guides you—while adjusting to your progress, schedule and other activities. With FitFusion Hub, you get access to four 4–6 week training plans. Whether you have access to a full gym or are training from the comfort of your home, there's a routine that fits you and your schedule so that you can reach your goals.

Basic Fitness focuses on fundamental exercises and principles to build strength, endurance, and flexibility. It's the perfect starting point for individuals of all fitness levels who want to establish a solid foundation for their health and fitness journey.

These exercises may include variations of squats, lunges, deadlifts, bench presses, rows, and overhead presses, utilizing free weights, machines, or bodyweight resistance.

  • Cardiovascular exercises
  • Focuses on fundamental fitness components
  • Builds muscle strength and endurance
  • Improves heart health and endurance
  • Promotes better posture and stability
